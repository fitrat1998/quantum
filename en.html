Dear colleagues!

We are pleased to announce the International Quantum Week, which will take place from December 10-14 in Samarkand, Tashkent, and Almalyk.

  • The opening of Quantum Week is scheduled for December 10 at Samarkand State University named after Sh. Rashidov.
  • On the first day, among the speakers will be Anvar Zakhidov, the scientific director of the Samarkand Quantum Center and a professor at the University of Texas in Dallas, and Ruslan Yusupov, co-founder of the Russian Quantum Center.
  • During this event, on December 10-11, lectures, workshops, and masterclasses will be held at Samarkand State University named after Sh. Rashidov, as well as at several schools in Samarkand. Invited experts will explain many complex concepts of quantum physics, quantum technologies, and quantum computers in simple terms.
  • The opening ceremony will take place at the conference hall of SamSU on December 10, 2024, at 10:00 AM, at 15 University Boulevard, Samarkand.
  • We look forward to seeing you at our event!
